Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a credit card to sign up?

No! You can start your 14-day free trial without entering any payment information. You only pay when you're ready to launch.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, absolutely. There are no long-term contracts for monthly plans. You can cancel your account at any time from your dashboard.

Is there a setup fee?

No, there are no setup fees. Our pricing is transparent, and you only pay the monthly or annual subscription fee.

Do you take transaction fees?

We do not charge transaction fees on our end. However, payment gateways (like Stripe or PayPal) typically charge a standard processing fee.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time directly from your account settings. Changes happen instantly.

Do you offer refunds?

If you're not satisfied within the first 30 days of your paid subscription, contact our support team for a full refund.
