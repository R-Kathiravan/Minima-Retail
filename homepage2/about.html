Why We Built Stackly

In 2020, we tried to open a small electronics shop. We were shocked by the software options available: clunky, ugly, expensive, and impossible to use without a computer science degree.

We believed that retail software should be as beautiful and considered as the products being sold.

So, we built the tool we wished we had. A platform that gets out of your way. No bloatware. No confusion. Just pure utility wrapped in a beautiful interface.
